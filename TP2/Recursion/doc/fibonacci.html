Fibonacci numbers¶

What are Fibonacci numbers?¶

Fibonacci numbers are a sequence of numbers defined by the two first terms :

\[\begin{split}f_0 &= 0\\ f_1 &= 1\\\end{split}\]

and for all \(n\geq 0\) by the following recursion relation :

\[f_{n+2} &= f_{n+1} + f_{n}.\]
